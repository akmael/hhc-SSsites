Gainesville: Home of the Gators!

Places to See

Kanapaha Botanical Gardens

A beautiful garden located on the west side of Gainesville, excellent for some meditation time.

Possum Creek Skate

A free-to-access skate parks located on the north side of town, featuring stairs and a full size bowl.

The Hippodrome

A major staple of Gainesville, The Hippodrome hosts a variety of plays, foreign films, and private partys.

Places to Eat

The Top: Restaurant

One of the "top" restaurants in Gainesville serving an excellent selection of food and drink.

Flaco's

A popular late night restaurant, perfect after a long night of Gainesville of dancing.

Sweet Mel's

A Bar/Resturant/Club. Known for its Funnel Cake Burger, and great drink specials.

Places to Go

The Atlantic

One of Gainesville's long standing music venues and also a favorite dance spot.

Barcade

The newest addition to downtown has become popular with the locals.

The Top: Bar

Token Tuesdays, Great food and indoor and outdoor bar!!! Whats not to love?
