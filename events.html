Events

Soul Cypher — Soul Cypher is the Hip-Hop Collective. Established in conjunction with the student organization, Soul Cypher is an independent Hip-Hop Festival that strives to represent the elements in their unity. Traditionally free of charge to the public and held with no prize money, the aim of the 3-day festival is to foster pride in the hip-hop arts and make those arts accessible to everyone.

    Main events:
  • The Crew Battle — a full-squad 8 vs 8 tournament to determine the best bboy crew in Florida
  • The Emcee Olympics - A series of events that pushes emcees (rappers) to utilize the full extent of their creativity, culminating in a battle.
  • The Poppin' battles — A 2 vs 2 competition that pays respect to the funk-styles influence in the culture
  • Graffiti Expo — An afternoon exhibition of Florida's street artists
  • Florida 7-to-Smoke — An invitational exhibition that puts the best upcoming bboys in the Southeast in a king of the hill style competition.

Icing on the Break — Started in 2002, Icing on the Break (IotB) is considered a staple in the Florida breaking scene. The main event is a 2 vs 2 breaking battle that is open to both students and the general public. Form & Funktion — F&F is the first popping focused event held in the North Florida area. Started in 2013, the goals of Form & Funktion are to promote interest and growth in the funk-styles dances that influence the Hip-Hop culture.

Nightmare of Beat Street — Nightmare on Beat Street is held in the Fall semester as a cypher event for all elements of hip-hop to come together without the need for structured competition. It is also an opportunity to give new DJs a start in the Florida community.
