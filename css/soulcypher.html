About Soul Cypher

Soul Cypher is a celebration of the four main elements of Hip Hop culture. It is a showcase of breaking, emceeing, DJing and graffiti art over three days. Since 2001, University of Florida students and alumni have come together as the Hip Hop Collective to create a positive space. For DJs, bboys, emcees, artists, and newcomers alike.

All Four Elements Represented

Soul Cypher aims to be a place for Hip Hop to thrive. A place where all guests can experience the urban arts in an empowering atmosphere. A place to enjoy all parts of the culture - the art, the dance and the music.

15th Anniversary

This year, Soul Cypher is proud to host its 15th Anniversary. We invite you to be part of a Florida tradition. This November, join us in creating the vibe, building our culture, and strengthening respect for the arts.
